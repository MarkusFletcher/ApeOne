Launch Plan

Pre mint

We will start by marking our territory in Solana and within the web3 community. We will initiate the recruiting process by opening our whitelist and start building the brand by establishing key partnerships for the tribe.

Mint

The minting process will be unfolding in two steps; The Ceremony, and The Celebration.

Only whitelisted future members of the tribe will be invited to The Ceremony where they will be able to purchase their APE ONE at an exclusive discounted price.

The Celebration is a public event where every APES will be coming together as ONE.

Post-mint

As the tribe is now working towards a common objective of executing the roadmap, hard work will be compensated with random airdrops of additional APE ONE within the community.
